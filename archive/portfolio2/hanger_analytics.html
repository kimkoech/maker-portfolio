Hanger Analytics

Description: A sensing device that uses an accelerometer to collect data from clothe hangers in retail stores
Date: Fall 2019
Location: Harvard
Software, Tools, Equipment: Arduino C, Digital and Analog electronics
Contributers: Billy Koech

Overview

An accelorometer is attached to an adafruit BLE microcontroller to collect acceleration and position data of any object attached onto the setup. The data is transmitted via bluetooth to a server device such as a phone as shown in the video below:
